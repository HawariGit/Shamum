Section I
The Canopy

From a tree that
keeps its own time

High in the mist, an ancient Aquilaria stands in soft cinematic light — its dense canopy decades in the making, its heartwood quietly preparing to form resin.

Section II
The Harvest

Read the trunk.
Draw the resin.

By hand and by patience, artisans cut into the dark, aromatic core — taking only the wood the tree has darkened. Side-light catches the glistening resin. The focus is the craft, never the volume.
