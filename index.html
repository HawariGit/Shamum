From Tree to Bottle

By hand
and by patience.

A century-old Aquilaria stands in mist-soft light, its heartwood quietly forming resin over decades. Our artisans read the trunk the way a physician reads a patient — by sight, scent, and touch.

Only 1 in 10 Aquilaria trees naturally produces the resinous heartwood we call Oud. We wait for the right tree. We wait for the right moment.
